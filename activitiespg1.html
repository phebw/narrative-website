Hop On Hop Off Bus Tour Of incredible Barcelona

This is a convenient, affordable way to see all the amazing landmarks and the vibrant culture of Barcelona. You can decide where to get off allowing you to see the landmarks you are most interested in. The tour bus also comes with headphones to learn about the history of the city, allowing you to learn about the rich history and culture of Barcelona.

Price:

€20 - €30

My recommendation:

Book with Barcelona Bus Turístic. I recommend the 48hr option as there is lots to see and there are 3 different routes you can go on. This also allows you to plan out your next few days in Barcelona better.
