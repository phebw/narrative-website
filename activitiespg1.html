Activities

What to do in Barcelona?

Barcelona is the capital of Spain so no wonder there is so much to do. As there is so many activities Barcelona is sure to accommodate to your needs and your budget. Here we will explore some activities you can do during and later in the day. All these activities are budget friendly to ensure you can enjoy as much of Barcelona as possible without compromising your budget. If you are a food lover, historian or outdoor person Barcelona is the place for you. Barcelona has something to offer everyone.

Hop On Hop Off Bus Tour

This is a convenient way to see all the amazing landmarks and the vibrant culture of Barcelona. You can decide where to get off allowing you to see the landmarks you are most interested in. The tour bus also comes with headphones to learn about the history of the city. This allows you to learn about the rich history and culture of Barcelona.

Price:

€20 - €30

My recommendation:

Book with Barcelona Bus Turístic. I recommend the 48hr option as there is lots to see and there are 3 different routes you can go on. This also allows you to plan out your next few days in Barcelona better.

FC Barcelona

If you are a football fan you cannot miss embarking on visiting the iconic FC Stadium, Camp Nou. Whether you visit when there is a game or not you will be amazed by this amazing stadium as it is the largest stadium in Europe. Home of the FC Barcelona football fans are guaranteed to enjoy this spectacular stadium.

Price:

Immersive Tour & Museum, from €28

Banana Boat

This is an unforgettable experience. If you love water sports, then this is the activity for you. Enjoy the panoramic views of Barcelona's iconic skyline if you can stay on the boat for that long. Enjoy the adrenaline rushing through your body as you bounce along the waves.

Price:

€20 - €25

My recommendation:

Try to book with a company that operates at Port Olympic, they have amazing views here as well as lots of bars and restaurants that you can go to after.

Cable Cars

Barcelona has breathtaking views and one convenient way to see them is on the cable cars. See the iconic landmarks from above such as the Sagrada Familia as well as the majestic sea views, this is not an activity to miss.

Price:

€13

Later in the day Activites

Flamenco Show

Experience Barcelona’s captivating culture with a flamenco show. Here you will experience the authentic and traditional culture of Barcelona. Flamenco is known for its passionate and wide range of emotions through its singers and dancers. This is one of Barcelona’s richest histories which includes Jewish, Gypsy and Moorish traditions This is not one to miss to really experience Barcelona culture.

Price:

Tickets from €20

My recommendation:

If watching an amazing flamenco show is not for you then how about taking a class? Barcelona offers flamenco classes with prices starting at €20.

Night Tour bus

Like the daytime hop-on-hop-off bus, you can see all the amazing lights of Barcelona's most iconic landmarks. Sit back, enjoy, and take hundreds of pictures of the incredible views you will see on this night bus tour. Choose to listen to the audio guide or simply play your own music and enjoy the exploration of Barcelona at nighttime.

Price:

€35

My recommendation:

I would advise going with the same company (Barcelona Bus Turístic) as the day tour bus as you can get a discount.

Night Clubs

Looking for cheaper clubs then head down to Espit Chupitos where you can get 600 different shots from just 2 euros each, this bar is the perfect place to drink before the club. Looking for another option, head down to the Mint. Here they serve cheap cocktails for you and your friends.

Price:

€10 - €20

My recommendation:

If you do not like these clubs utilise there cheaper drinks as pres. After this go to the more expensive clubs. But be safe!

Night Clubs Part 2

Head down to Marítim de la Barceloneta where you will find the likes of clubs such as Shoko, Pacha and Opium. Although these clubs are on the pricier side you have the potential to see celebrities and have celebrity performances. Previous celebrities have included: Lil T-jay, Tyga, Central Cee and many more. So, the price could be worth seeing these celebrities.

Price:

€30 - €50

My recommendation:

Try to book the clubs in advance, they can be as low as €10 the day before and morning off so be sure to act promptly. Also, to ensure you stay on a low budget buy drinks from corner shops as a simple vodka lemonade can cost €15 euros in these clubs.

A way to spend 4 days in Barcelona using this travel guide.

  • Day 1

    Tour bus

    Hop on the tour bus stay on the tour bus for one whole round trip to see where you would like to go. Go again and explore your favourite places

  • Day 2

    Any activity

    Further explore the tourist attractions from the bus tour especially the cable cars

  • Day 3

    Any Activity

    Choose one of the many activities shown above such as the banana boat, flamenco show, football stadium etc.

  • Day 4

    Rest Day

    Chill out at the beach and catch a tan ready to return looking amazing!
