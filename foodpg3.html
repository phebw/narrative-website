FC Camp Nou. Barcelona Football Fan Anyone?

If you are a football fan you cannot miss embarking on visiting the iconic FC Stadium, Camp Nou. Whether you visit when there is a game or not you will be amazed by this amazing stadium as it is the largest stadium in Europe. Home of the FC Barcelona football fans are guaranteed to enjoy this spectacular stadium.

Price:

Immersive Tour & Museum, from €28

Banana Boat Try your best not to fall

This is an unforgettable experience. If you love water sports, then this is the activity for you. Enjoy the panoramic views of Barcelona's iconic skyline if you can stay on the boat for that long. Enjoy the adrenaline rushing through your body as you bounce along the waves.

Price:

€20 - €25

My recommendation:

Try to book with a company that operates at Port Olympic, they have amazing views here as well as lots of bars and restaurants that you can go to after.

Cable Cars Experience panoramic views of Barcelona

Barcelona has breathtaking views and one convenient way to see them is on the cable cars. See the iconic landmarks from above such as the Sagrada Familia as well as the majestic sea views, this is not an activity to miss.

Price:

€13
