Tapas Selection

Tapas is a traditional style of dining that includes small dishes. There is a variety of tapas you can get, and some are identified here there are lots more so be sure to visit a dedicated tapas restaurant when in Barcelona to see all the amazing food Barcelona has to offer.

Croquettes

These are oval shaped snacks with a creamy filling inside with ingredients such as meat, potatoes, ham, fish etc. This is then coated in breadcrumbs and deep fried. What‚Äôs not to love?

Sardines

Sardines is another popular Spanish tapa that can be served in many ways. It can be served on bread as a croquette, it can be grilled, it can be placed on a skewer and lots of other ways.

Mussels

This is a popular seafood choice in Spain. These can be served in a variety of ways such as Mussels in White Wine Sauce, Mussels with Tomato and Chorizo, Mussels in Garlic and Parsley Sauce etc.

Calamari

This is a delicious squid which is deep fried and coated in a light batter. This can be served with lemon or garlic mayonnaise for dipping. This is a great dish for fish lovers out there.

Empanadas

This can be considered a traditional tapa to some extent but this is a Savoury turnover which can have different fillings such as meat, vegetables, cheese, seafood, or a combination of ingredients.

Patatas Bravas

This is a classic Spanish loved dish that you cannot miss on your trip to Barcelona. This dish consists of crispy fried potatoes with a spicy tomatoes sauce poured on top. There are even variations with different sauces or garlic mayonnaise on the side. This is frequently enjoyed by friends and family in Spain and is a common tapas staple.
