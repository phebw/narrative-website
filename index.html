Why you should visit Barcelona?

This guide will be your ultimate help as to why and how you should spend your time in Barcelona. From it’s history and architecture to it’s culture and nightlife. Barcelona has everything to offer for you. This guide will include my recommendations for accommodation, activities, food and transport to help you make the best decisions when exploring Barcelona. Barcelona is a vibrant city that you are bound to fall in love with. It caters for all but especially young adults. The abundance of activities and insane nightlife will make you never want to leave this enchanting city. Barcelona has such diverse neighbourhoods and incredible cultures. It’s landmarks such as Sagrada Família and Park Güell are unforgettable experiences you have to see. So come along and lets help you plan your trip to Barcelona.

Tips For Your Trip To Barcelona

Plan in advance especially if going in the summer months!

Barcelona is a popular place for not only young adults but adults! As spontaneous and flexible as you may want to be its important you book one or two things in advance to ensure you see all the beautiful things Barcelona has to offer.

Create a budget and try your best to stick to it!

Its easy to be enticed by all the activities you can do in Barcelona although you should enjoy it as much as possible try not to overspend and always look around for cheaper alternatives.

Wear comfortable shoes!

You will end up doing lots of walking especially when trying to save money on transport so ensure you wear appropriate footwear.

Beware of pickpockets!

Especially on public transport they are very sneaky with it so keep your eyes pealed.
