Barcelona

For young adults on a budget

Why you should visit Barcelona

Barcelona is one of the most popular destinations for young adults. From it's party scene to amazing tourist attractions. Barcelona is the place to be.

Tips For Your Trip To Barcelona

Plan in advance especially if going in the summer months! Barcelona is a popular place for not only young adults but adults! As spontaneous and flexible as you may want to be its important you book one or two things in advance to ensure you see all the beautiful things Barcelona has to offer. Create a budget and try your best to stick to it! Its easy to be enticed by all the activities you can do in Barcelona although you should enjoy it as much as possible try not to overspend and always look around for cheaper alternatives. Wear comfortable shoes You will end up doing lots of walking especially when trying to save money on transport so ensure you wear appropriate footwear. And please do not forget sunscreen. Beware of pickpockets Especially on public transport they are very sneaky with it so keep your eyes pealed.
