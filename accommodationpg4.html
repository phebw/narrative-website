Accommodation

Where to stay in Barcelona?

Barcelona is a popular destination for tourists. This means there are lots of hostels, hotels, apartments etc. . The trouble is trying to find a budget friendly place located in the centre and one that satisfies your needs. It's important to be located as central as possible to ensure you do not have to spend lots of money on transport. Opting for a cheaper option may offset the money you save on accommodation because of this. There are several budget friendly options which will be explored. Hostels

Hostels can seem daunting; sharing your space with others can be frightening, however if you find the right one it can make your Barcelona experience. A hostel can include sharing showers, bathrooms and kitchens. Some, however, can just include sharing a kitchen. If you are a solo traveler this is a great way to make friends and have people to come explore Barcelona with you.
